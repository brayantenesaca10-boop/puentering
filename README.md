# Puente - A Visibility Company

## What Is Puente?

Puente makes invisible people visible.

Every day, millions of people go to work, raise families, and live extraordinary lives that nobody around them knows about. A janitor who coached soccer for 20 years. A receptionist who immigrated from Ecuador, earned a CPA license, and bought her own house. A teacher who paints on weekends. They are surrounded by people but not seen.

Puente changes that. Starting with a ring.

## The Belief

Every person has a story worth knowing, and the world is structured to make sure nobody hears it. We're changing that.

## Why This Exists

Stories go untold. A mother's journey from Ecuador to the US, from cleaning McDonald's bathrooms to university, to a CPA license, to buying her own house. No one talks about it. No one knows it. But she is dying to share it.

The tools that exist - social media, business cards, networking apps - are built for people who are already visible. Nobody is building for the people who are overlooked. The janitor. The teacher. The receptionist. The immigrant. The person who is right there, every single day, and nobody asks.

Puente is for them.

## What Puente Is NOT

- Not a networking tool
- Not a digital business card
- Not a smart ring company
- Not a tech gadget

Puente is a visibility company. The ring is the first product. It won't be the last.

## The Product: Puente Ring

A ceramic ring with an NFC chip sealed inside. Tap it on any phone. Your story appears - in their language, in your voice.

### How It Works

1. Record your story - open the app and talk. Your name, your hometown, your craft, your passions. AI clones your voice and speaks your words in any language. Not a robot. You.

2. Tap - hold your ring near any phone. No app needed on their side. Your profile appears - your story, your voice, in their language. Three seconds.

3. See the bridge - shared interests light up. You both play soccer. You're both from Ecuador. You both have kids at the same school. What was invisible becomes visible. You instantly see WHY you should talk to this person.

### The Moment

When that janitor taps his ring and someone sees his story, his interests, the AI shows them they both love soccer, they both came from Ecuador, they both have a daughter the same age - he didn't just "connect." He became visible. For maybe the first time in that building.

People will forget every spec on that ring. They will never forget the first time someone actually saw them.

## Brand Identity

### Name: Puente
- Pronounced: "PWEN-teh"
- Meaning: Bridge (Spanish)
- A Spanish name for a product built for people who have been overlooked. Authentic, not performative.

### Tagline
"Every person has a story worth knowing"

### The Enemy
Invisibility. Being surrounded but not seen.

### Brand Language
- Never say "networking." Say: be seen, be heard, tell your story
- Never say "digital business card." Say: your story, your voice, your people
- Never say "smart ring." Say: the ring that carries your story
- Ping is a tool. Puente is a movement.

### Color Palette
- Primary: Matte black + warm white
- Accent: Warm terracotta (cultural, luxury - NOT tech blue)
- This says dignity + warmth. Not Silicon Valley.

### Logo Concept
- The word "Puente" with the "u" and "e" connected by a subtle arc/bridge line
- Or a minimal bridge icon - two dots connected by an arc (represents two people bridged)
- Clean, one color, works embossed on packaging, engraved on ring

### The Sound
- Custom notification sound when someone connects with you
- Warm, resonant - like a soft chime or wooden knock
- This becomes the sonic brand

### Ring Design
- Zirconia ceramic (ZrO2), evaluating matte black and glossy black
- 5mm band width - looks like jewelry, not a gadget
- Beveled edges on both sides
- Clean exterior - no logos, no text, no branding on the outside
- "PUENTE" laser engraved on inner band (hidden detail)

### Packaging
- Magnetic closure box, matte black, soft-touch exterior
- "Puente" logo debossed on top (no ink, just pressed into the material)
- Inside: ring on a small cushion/foam insert
- Card underneath: "Your story matters."

## Core Features

### 1. Voice Story Profiles
- Tap the ring, get a rich profile with your story told in YOUR voice
- AI voice cloning in both English and Spanish (or Portuguese, Creole, etc.)
- Listener picks their language, hears YOUR voice telling YOUR story in THEIR language
- Voice-first input: record your profile by talking, AI transcribes and translates

### 2. Visibility Engine
- When you connect with someone, you visually see what connects you
- Shared interests, shared hometowns, shared experiences light up
- Makes the invisible visible - you instantly see WHY you should talk to this person

### 3. Conversation Starters
- Based on shared interests, the app generates conversation starters
- "You both played soccer growing up - ask about their favorite team"
- "You're both from Guayaquil - ask what neighborhood"
- Removes the awkwardness, especially across language barriers

### 4. Community Discovery
- "Who else in Danbury is from Guayaquil?"
- "Who speaks Portuguese and does plumbing?"
- Local directory that grows organically through taps
- Churches, community centers, and local businesses become hubs

### 5. Trust Signals
- Endorsements from community members, church leaders, employers
- "Maria vouches for this person" carries real weight
- Social proof built the way these communities actually build trust - through relationships

## The Decision Filter

Every decision runs through one filter: does this make invisible people more visible?

If yes, do it. If no, cut it.

The finish on the ring? Pick whichever one feels like it belongs on the hand of someone who has been overlooked their whole life and is about to be seen for the first time.

The packaging? It shouldn't feel like tech. It should feel like someone handing you something and saying "your story matters."

The website? Don't lead with features. Lead with the human truth that started this.
