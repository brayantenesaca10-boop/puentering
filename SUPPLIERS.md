## Where to Find Suppliers

### Tier 1: Alibaba (Start Here)

Best for: First batch, competitive pricing, most NFC ring manufacturers are here.

**How to search:**
- Go to alibaba.com
- Search: "custom NFC ceramic ring NTAG216"
- Also try: "NFC smart ring ceramic custom logo"
- Also try: "zirconia ceramic NFC ring OEM"

**What to look for in a listing:**
- "Verified Supplier" or "Gold Supplier" badge
- Trade Assurance available (payment protection)
- 2+ years on the platform
- Photos of actual ceramic NFC rings (not renders)
- Reviews from other buyers mentioning NFC rings specifically
- Response rate above 80%

**Suppliers to check (known NFC ring manufacturers):**
- Search for shops based in Shenzhen or Dongguan - that's where most NFC ring factories are
- Filter by "Trade Assurance" for payment protection
- Look at supplier profiles that show factory photos (not just trading companies)

**Red flags to avoid:**
- No factory photos (they're a middleman, not a manufacturer)
- Won't send samples
- Price seems too good to be true (under $1.50/ring for ceramic)
- Can't answer basic questions about NTAG216 vs NTAG213
- No experience with ceramic specifically (metal rings are a different process)

### Tier 2: Made-in-China.com

Same manufacturers as Alibaba, sometimes with different pricing. Worth cross-referencing.

- Search: "ceramic NFC ring custom"
- Same vetting process as Alibaba

### Tier 3: DHgate

More retail-oriented but some manufacturers sell direct. Good for finding suppliers who do smaller MOQs.

### Tier 4: Domestic / US-Based

Higher cost but faster shipping and easier communication.

- **CNICK** (cnick.com) - US-based NFC ring company, may do white-label
- **RingMii** - Another NFC ring company worth reaching out to
- **Etsy manufacturers** - Search "custom NFC ring manufacturer" - some sellers do B2B

### Tier 5: Direct Factory Contact

If you find a ring brand you like (Cnick, Hecere, Jakcom), email them directly and ask if they do OEM/white-label. Many of these brands ARE the factory.

## Vetting Process (Do This for Every Supplier)

### Step 1: First Contact
- Send the email template above to 5-10 suppliers
- Note who responds within 24 hours (good sign) vs 3+ days (bad sign)

### Step 2: Compare Quotes

### Step 3: Request Samples
- Order 1-2 samples from your top 2-3 suppliers ($5-15 per sample + $10-20 shipping)
- Total sample cost: ~$30-60 for peace of mind
- Test: NFC read range, finish quality, comfort, engraving quality, sizing accuracy

### Step 4: Sample Evaluation Checklist
- [ ] NFC chip reads reliably with iPhone and Android
- [ ] Read range is at least 1-2cm
- [ ] Matte finish is consistent, no glossy spots
- [ ] No visible seams or chip bumps
- [ ] Comfortable to wear for 8+ hours
- [ ] Engraving is clean and centered
- [ ] Ring matches specified dimensions
- [ ] Waterproof (dunk in water for 10 minutes, test NFC after)

### Step 5: Place Order
- Use Trade Assurance on Alibaba if possible
- Pay via the platform, not wire transfer (for buyer protection)
- Get written confirmation of specs, quantity, delivery date
- Ask for photos of the rings before they ship (production photos)

## Key Questions to Ask Yourself Before Ordering

1. **Design finalized?** - Don't order until you've picked your one differentiator (gold accent, groove, beveled edge, etc.). Changing design mid-production costs money.
2. **Samples received?** - Never place a 100-unit order without testing a sample first.
3. **Payment protection?** - Use Trade Assurance or PayPal. Never wire transfer to a new supplier.
4. **Shipping timeline?** - Standard shipping from China is 15-25 days. Express is 5-10 days but costs 3-4x more. Plan accordingly.
5. **Backup supplier?** - Have a second supplier quoted in case your first choice falls through.
